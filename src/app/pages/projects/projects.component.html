I like calisthenics. I love the simplicity of training in this way. I often combine my workout with being in the nature, doing sprints on the lonely forest roads or pullups on the branches (although it takes some time to find these horizontal branches of appropiate diameters).

Being exposed, free in a vast open area helps me think and I often come back home with some game-changing ideas. For instance, the idea to use a memory game to introduce myself was a result of such an act.
